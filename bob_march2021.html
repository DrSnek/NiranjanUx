Bob Moss

Bob Moss is a world-renowned Squagish artist, and has been know to recreate many extremely popular Pengaissance arts. He has graduated from Elizfish Flipward High School. He used to also practice carving and molding, but soon after focused his talents on painting. He makes for a top-class designer for us at NiranjanUx (For the penguins, by the penguins)
