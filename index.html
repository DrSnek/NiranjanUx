What does NiranjanUx do?

NiranjanUx is an exclusive kernel provider. We believe in equality here at NiranjanUx, so to make up for the penguins' loss of habitat, we've given them a fully functioning Linux kernel. By the penguins, for the penguins.

Why NiranjanUx?

Imagine a life without computers. Now imagine a life without a home. Now put them together. That's a penguin for you. NiranjanUx takes help from these penguins to build the kernel, as nothing understands penguin needs better than penguins.

How can I help?

As a penguin comrade, you can do nothing but help us in this vast project! If you don't know coding, help us with suggestions on what penguins would like to see in the kernel. If you know coding, of course, then help create NiranjanUx. No slavery involved
